Aelaan-E-Jung 8th February

Aelaan-E-Jung is an inter college dance competition where enthusiastic dancers from all over Mumbai compete for their glory. All the event coordinators work in harmony to satisfy the demands of the participants and synchronise effectively for better execution of event. The winner of the competition is finally decided by Judges, and the participants are judged based on their creativity, expression, style and attitude. The event gives a platform for emerging dance groups to showcase their talent. But at last only one wins the title and hearts of millions with their talent.

Sargam Finals : 7th February

SARGAM is an Intercollegiate solo and duet singing competition curated for budding vocalists. Come forth to exhibit your talent in diverse genres of music before an esteemed panel of judges with an amazing opportunity to take part in an enthralling online contest. Winners of the event and the online contest will be felicitated with exciting gifts and prizes.

War of Branches 8th February

Love your branch? Want to see it bask in the glory of winning the SPIT CUP? You've come to the right event. War of Branches is a dance competition among the five branches of our very own college, in which anyone who loves dancing gives it all for their branch! Win it and get a step closer to being the best branch of the year! Not just that, you hold a chance to get exciting vouchers too!
